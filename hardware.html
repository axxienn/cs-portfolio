Hardware

Motherboard is a computer's central in which allows different parts of the computer to be gathered at one place and communicate with each other. Essentially, it looks like a mini contraption on plastic board. The different components would potentially include a CPU, RAM, multipole USB ports, ect. It is also known as "the heart of the computer".
